YOU'RE IN AMERICA,
SPEAK ENGLISH

A phrase both my parents and grandparents have heard one too many times.

The world has asked me to "Americanize" myself and yet:

THE REFUSAL TO HIDE MY CULTURE HAS BECOME MY GREATEST WEAPON.




It's hard to navigate

food shame,
ethnic feature shame,
and language shame.
