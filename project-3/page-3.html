ETHNIC FEATURE SHAME

I DON'T CARE THAT
EUROCENTRIC FEATURES
HAVE SET THE BEAUTY
STANDARD.

I just don't understand why we have to hate
the features that don't fit into this standard. I
have been taught to hate my wide nose, my small &
monolid eyes, my yellow-tinted skin.

My features have been carried through generations. My features come from two strong and resilient families. They have traveled across the sea, to a new country, learned a new language, a new culture. I have my mother's nose, my father's eyebrows, my great-grandma's dimple, my grandfather's height, & my great-aunt's hair. I am a genetic collage of the people who have sacrificed for me, who have loved me.

MY FACE HAS A HISTORY BEHIND IT. HOW COULD I HATE THAT?
